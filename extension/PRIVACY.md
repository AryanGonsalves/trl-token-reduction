# TRL Composer — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026_

**Short version: TRL Composer does not collect, store, sell, or transmit your data
to us or any third party. Your text never leaves your own computer.**

## What the extension does
When you click **Compress**, the text currently in the Claude.ai message box is
compressed so it uses fewer tokens. The compression happens in one of two places,
both under your control:

1. **Built-in (default):** the text is compressed entirely inside your browser by
   the extension's own code. It is never sent anywhere.
2. **Local engine (optional):** if you have chosen to run the companion engine on
   your own machine (`http://localhost:8899`), the text is sent only to that
   local address, on your own computer, and the result is returned. Nothing is
   sent over the internet.

## What we collect
Nothing. There are no analytics, no tracking, no remote servers operated by us,
no accounts, and no advertising identifiers. The extension makes no network
requests to any server we control.

## Permissions and why
- **`storage`** — to remember your local setting (e.g. the optional engine
  address). Stored only in your browser.
- **Access to `claude.ai`** — to place the Compress button and read/replace the
  text in the message box, only when you click the button.
- **Access to `localhost:8899`** — only to reach the optional local engine on your
  own machine, if you run it. Never a remote address.

## Your control
The extension never sends a message for you. It only compresses text and shows you
a preview; you decide whether to use it and you press Enter yourself.
